FAQ
01
What is an AI Node?
An AI node is a license to contribute into our decentralized AI network through data, memory, compute and verification, thus making you a part of Bluwhale’s AI brain.
02
How do I set up and run an AI Node?
Download the Bluwhale mobile app by connecting your wallet on www.bluwhale.com and then you can set up your account and operate the nodes with a single click
03
What are the benefits of running an AI Node?
The Master nodes allow you to contribute to AI verfication of the data coming into our network, and on top of that you can create a community of friends to operate nodes together to earn tokens
04
What are the mobile requirements for operating an AI Node?
We support all mobile devices both iOS, Android and other mobile operating systems. The Bluwhale app does not consume much battery or compute on your device and runs in the background with all other apps
05
How are rewards distributed to AI Node owners?
Rewards are distributed through your Bluwhale profile where you can earn and monitor the token emissions of your nodes.
06
Can I purchase and run multiple AI Nodes?
Yes, you can. There is no limitation of how many nodes you can run on your mobile device. Once you connected to the Bluwhale platform with your node purchase wallet, we automatically recognize the number of AI nodes and allow you to click and run them all at once.
